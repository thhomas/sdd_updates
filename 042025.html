Stat Of The Week

 
 

In 2023, Samoa's current account balance reached 40 million USD, reflecting the nation's trade and financial flows with the world.  Tracking Pacific statistics like these is key to understanding economic health and international relationships. More info

 

In 2020, 86% of American Samoa’s land area was covered by forest, a vital resource for biodiversity and climate regulation.  With International Mother Earth Day this week, let’s celebrate and protect the natural treasures that sustain our planet. More info

 

For World Health Day, we highlight healthcare access in Tonga. Among the 37 healthcare facilities in the country, 20 are located in Tongatapu, showing the concentration of services in the main island.  Data from the Pacific Data Hub: pacificdata.org The mural pictured is in Nuku’alofa by Taniela Petelo - Seleka, a young Tongan artist. More info
