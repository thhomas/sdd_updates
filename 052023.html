Stat Of The Week

 
 

Evolution of the average price per kilo for Yellow fin Tuna (whole, from Pacific Ocean) between 2014 and 2021, in US dollars.   2nd of May is World Tuna Day. This fish provides food and incomes for many people in the Pacific, and is essential to livelihoods in the region. Yellowfin can live up to six or seven years. They are highly migratory and are found throughout the Pacific, Atlantic and Indian Oceans (WWF).   Explore more data from the Pacific Data Hub (PDH) here.     More info

 

13 births per 1000 population. This is the crude birth rate in French Polynesia in 2020.  Let's discover more about Births Statistics on the Population Dashboard.   Explore more data from the Pacific Data Hub (PDH) here. More info

 

Papua New Guinea is the biggest producer of coconuts in the Pacific, around 1,780,312 tonnes.   Known regionally as the ‘tree of life’, every part is used, from its tallest frond down to its rooted trunk – from the sweet, delectable juice and fresh meat of its fruits, to the husks to fuel fires, fronds to make baskets, and the trunks for building homes and furniture.   Explore more data fom the Pacific Data Hub (PHD) here.      More info
