Stat Of The Week

 
 

Did you know?  Guam’s score on the Control of Corruption Index was 1.25 in 2023, reflecting effective governance with some room for improvement.  This indicator measures how well public power is safeguarded from corruption, a key factor for transparency and sustainable development.  Find more data here.  More info
