Stat Of The Week

 
 

In Palau, access to adapted infrastructure and learning materials for students with disabilities in primary schools has improved in recent years. In 2020, 84% of primary schools were equipped. In 2021, 100% of primary schools met the standard. More info

 

In the Solomon Islands, forests covered around 90% of the total land area in 2020 (SDG indicator 15.1.1 – Life on Land). More info

 

In the Federated States of Micronesia, the proportion of women in the national parliament (SDG 5.5.1a) has nearly tripled over three years, increasing from 7% in 2023 to 21% in 2025. More info
