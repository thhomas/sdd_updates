Stat Of The Week

 
 

In 2023, Tokelau recorded a trade balance of –$6.501 million USD, with imports ($6.508 million USD) far exceeding exports ($7,000 USD). More info

 

In Fiji, gross tourism earnings in 2024 reached $1.118 billion USD, highlighting the sector’s major contribution to the national economy. More info

 

In 2024, Samoa recorded the highest yield of mango, mangosteen and guava in the region, reaching 34,621 kg per hectare. More info
